Peak Localization: The approximate coordinates of major peaks were identified through the broad-mode scan.

Peak Refinement: Subsequently, the fine-mode scan was utilized to pinpoint the precise location and value of these peaks.

Statistical Validation: To rigorously determine the accuracy of the identified FSS values, statistical resampling techniques, specifically bootstrap and jackknife methods, were performed on the fine-mode scan data. These procedures were employed to estimate the uncertainty of the peak values and to ensure the robustness of the final results against sampling bias.


The FSS values were calculated by aggregating the fine-mode dataset, followed by statistical validation using bootstrap (N iterations) and jackknife (leave-one-out) procedures to establish confidence intervals.
